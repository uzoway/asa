Asa is a Nigerian-French singer, songwriter,
and recording artist with a smoky voice. She combines
elements of soul, pop, folk, and reggae, among other
international influences, in her warm neo-soulsongs. She
had her debut album in 2008 to critical acclaim has since
had 3 of her songs debuting at No 3 in GRAMMY world
music ranking chart.

her stage name asa, meaning
'hawk' was inspired when she
